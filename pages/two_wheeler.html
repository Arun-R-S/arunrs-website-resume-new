Anti-Theft Two Wheeler Security System

Arduino

Due to the increasing vehicle theft cases all over the world vehicle security system has more value in the automobile market. Most of the best suited advanced security system is available only for two wheelers, but the system available for two wheelers in the market are of no match to the well-equipped thieves. We are proposing an Anti-Theft protection to your bike with Bike tracker and GPS Bike Security System. Two-wheeler Anti-theft security system detects unauthorized ignition / movement. Track your bike with anti-theft tracking system when it is stolen.

When Theft is detected you can switch off your bike engine just by sending SMS to your bikeâ€™s security system. Every security system has a Unique ID which is helpful to find your different vehicle location. You can get an immediate alert SMS if your bike is stolen by Theft. You will be getting information about your bike to your mobile on ignition detection, movement detection, you can Switch Off engine via mobile phone, Vehicle location identification is send via SMS (GPRS enabled Sim) on mobile phone, GSM location helps you to track the bike. Our Bike Security System also detect the fuel theft and make an alert to you through SMS. GPS tracker communicates with your mobile phone via SMS. You need a mobile with Google Map for tracking your bike.

This Vehicle tracking device can be installed in all types of vehicle irrespective of model and make. It is very comfortable for installing in any type of vehicle.
